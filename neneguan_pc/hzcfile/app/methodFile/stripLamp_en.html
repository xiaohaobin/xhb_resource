The way to deal with the failure of the remote control of LED Strip Lamp

Dear user, we are sorry for your inconvenience. You can adjust the remote control in the following ways.

First of all: check whether the plastic insulator at the battery port of the remote control has been taken out. Please take out the plastic insulator before trying to control it.

Check whether the battery is installed normally: according to the instructions on the back of the remote control, take out the battery from the remote control and restore it again, keep the power supply of the remote control, and then try to control the light band.

When the remote control is not used for a long time, please keep it in a cool and dry place to avoid moisture or battery loss.
